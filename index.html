our practice area

Immigration

At Magbegor & associates, we are aware of the level of stress and anxiety involved when navigating the complex immigration process. We are experienced and knowledgeable in handling all aspects of immigration petitions, from your original visa application, residence permits, and filing for US citizenship, to even appealing a negative decision from the U.S Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS).  

Family-Based Immigration

I-130, Waivers, Adjustment of Status, Consular Process, Citizenship, VAWA, U-Visa

Employment-Based Immigration

Employment-Based Immigration ,Adjustment of Status, H-1B, L-1A, L-1B, O-1, P-1, EAD, F-1/M-1

Removal Defence

Cancellation of Removal Non-legal permanent resident,  Cancellation of Removal Legal Permanent Resident, Voluntary Departure, Asylum, Withholding of Removal, Bond

Employment

We represents employees and employers of all sizes in matters concerning business and employment law. We will work with you on employment disputes ranging from workplace discrimination, retaliation claims, sexual harassment claims, whistleblower cases, wrongful termination, unpaid wages, severance negotiation, and numerous other employment disputes.

We also work with you in the formation of your business entity.

This includes business contracts, lease agreements, employee handbooks and policies, investigating employment disputes, and protecting assets and trade secrets.

Civil Litigation

We provides legal representation to individuals and businesses seeking trial lawyers with the highest level of skill, commitment, experience, and resources. We will work with you to gather the facts of the case, assess the nature and circumstances surrounding the dispute, research the law of the case, and advice regarding the cost-benefit of prosecuting or defending the case in federal court or state court.
