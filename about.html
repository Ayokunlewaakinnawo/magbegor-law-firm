Serving Dallas-Fort Worth and Oklahoma with Excellence in Legal Representation. Magbegor Law Firm is a distinguished law firm dedicated to providing high-quality legal services to individuals and businesses throughout the Dallas-Fort Worth area and Oklahoma. We understand the complexities and challenges our clients face, and we are committed to delivering results with skill, experience, and personalized attention.

Our Mission and Values

we are driven by a simple yet powerful mission: to provide high-caliber legal representation that is both effective and accessible.

Zino Magbegor

Founder & Lead Lawyer

An experienced employment litigator representing management in defense of claims brought under various state and federal employment laws. She is skilled at litigating and managing complex class action and collective action matters related to discrimination, retaliation, harassment, and wage and hour disputes.

Tiffany also assists employers with the proactive implementation of sound employment practices and counsels clients on litigation avoidance.
